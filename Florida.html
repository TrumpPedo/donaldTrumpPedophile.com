1. You MUST be registered to vote! You can do that online RIGHT HERE. To register, you have to have either a Florida ID or Florida Driver's license. If you need to get a Florida ID, look HERE for the stuff you need to take with you to the DMV.
2. You have to request a vote-by-mail ballot. There are a lot of ways to do it outlined here. You can do it online, but you have to find your specific county Supervisor of Elections. It takes about 15 minutes, don't worry! Start HERE. Pick your county, it will provide a link to your county's website. For example, if you're in Miami-Dade, your website is: https://www.miamidade.gov/global/elections/home.page. When you get to that page, look for a link to request your mail-in ballot. Remember, Florida is a red state, and that means the politicians will try to make this confusing for you. Don't give up!
3. When you receive your vote-by-mail ballot, carefully follow the directions and return it as directed AS SOON AS POSSIBLE!
That too much? Ok, no problem ... vote early! Find out how to do that in Florida HERE.
